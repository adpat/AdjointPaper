\frametitle{Riemann problem}

Define a \textbf{Riemann problem} as a Cauchy problem:

\begin{equation}
\begin{cases}
\cvar_{t}+f\left(\cvar\right)_{x} & =0\\
\cvar\left(0,x\right) & =\cvar_{0}\left(x\right)
\end{cases}
\end{equation}

where:

\begin{equation}
\cvar_{0}\left(x\right) = 
\begin{cases}
\cvar_l & x < 0 \\ 
\cvar_r & x \ge 0 \\ 
\end{cases}
\end{equation}
